The Kacchi Ghani Process

Preserving the legacy of traditional Indian oil extraction.

No Heat. No Chemicals.

At Neejhar Milap Oil Mills, we don't just extract oil; we preserve a legacy. Industrial processing uses high heat that destroys the soul of the seed. Our traditional wooden presses operate at room temperature.

This painstaking, slow process ensures that every drop of Neejhar oil retains its raw nutritional profile, vital antioxidants, and the robust aroma that has been the cornerstone of Indian kitchens for centuries.
